How to get your desired WAM

Enter your current WAM (use the WAM calculator if you don't know it), your desired WAM, how many UOC you have taken, as well as how many UOC are left in your degree. Press the button to calculate the average mark you will have to achieve per subject to achieve your desired WAM.

Note: WAM has a minimum of 0 and maximum of 100.
